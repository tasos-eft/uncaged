HOW TO ENTER


The Promotion will take place at uncage.ie (the "Website").


No purchase of any product is required to enter the Promotion.


To enter the Promotion you must log on to uncage.ie to claim. By logging on to uncage.ie, entrants are consenting to the terms and conditions of the website. When logging on to uncage.ie for the first time, entrants will be asked to register their first name, last name, email address and mobile number where they can choose to redeem a complimentary pint. Once they are fully signed up, entrants will be able to claim their complimentary pint of Tiger from participating pubs.


Once an entrant has set up their account and verified their mobile number using the 4 digit code sent to their device, they’ll be able to claim a pint of Tiger. They will need to use their personal mobile device to redeem their pint in the pubs. In order to redeem their pint, the entrant must tap on the redeem button in the Wallet section of uncage.ie. The entrant will then be asked to choose a participating pub. Once the entrant has selected the participating pub, the entrant must enter confirm that they’re in a participating pub (or the nearest participating pub will be shown on their screen) and they will have 120 seconds to claim their complimentary pint. It is therefore important that the entrant follows the instructions on the screen and only press the redeem button once they’ve ordered their pint with the barman in the participating pub.


At midnight every day for the duration of the Competition Period there will be an allocation of Tiger pints that can be claimed by people registering on the website. There will be a total of 8,000 pints available to be claimed during the pint Redemption Period. The number of pints allocated daily and the time of release is at the discretion of the Promoter. When an entrant registers on the website, they will be able to claim a complimentary pint of Tiger, provided they are eligible to do so. The pint is stored in their wallet and the pool of complimentary pints for that day reduces by 1. This is limited to the amount of complimentary pints available that day and is managed on a first come first served basis. The complimentary pint can be redeemed at one of the on-trade outlets listed in the Participating venues section of the uncage.ie website. Heineken is not responsible for incorrect placement of a Participating Pub on the map function of the uncage.ie microsite.


Once a complimentary pint of Tiger has been awarded within the uncage.ie site, entrants will have 7 days to redeem in a participating pub. If a pint is not redeemed within 7 days, it will expire and be removed from the entran’ts wallet. Entrants are only able to store one complimentary pint in their dashboard at any one time.


Participating venues in which the pint or coupon is being redeemed may require proof of age. Entry into the participating venue is at the sole discretion of the participating outlet or accountvenue. Service of the complimentary pint is at the sole discretion of the participating outletvenue.


Complimentary pints and coupons are non-transferable. The pint and coupon will have no monetary value. Only a pint of Tiger can be redeemed, no other products are redeemable.


A complimentary pint and money off coupon is void if not redeemed in accordance with these Terms & Conditions and/or is obtained, altered or otherwise used fraudulently or improperly in Heineken's sole opinion. Multiple registrations by a consumer are not permitted and will not be valid. A maximum of two (2) pints per person can be redeemed in total throughout the campaign. However the promoter may contact the entrant via the email address or mobile number registered, at a later date, to offer an additional complimentary pint.
